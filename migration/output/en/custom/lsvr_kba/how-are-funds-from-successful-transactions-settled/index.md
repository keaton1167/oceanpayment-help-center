---
title: "How are funds from successful transactions settled?"
date: 2025-05-30
---

Upon fulfillment of the contractual settlement conditions, Oceanpayment will transfer the agreed settlement funds to the merchant's contracted bank account or Opasst settlement platform.

 

If the contracted account is a bank account, you can check the corresponding transferred funds in the bank account.

If you have signed with Opasst, you can login to the corresponding system to withdraw funds or make payment on behalf of the merchant.
