---
title: "I forgot my ODPM login password, how do I reset it?"
sidebar_label: "I forgot my ODPM login password, how do I reset it?"
sidebar_position: 2
---
If you have forgotten the Administrator login password for your account, please contact your dedicated Operations Support Manager to reset the password.

If you have forgotten the password of the sub-accounts under your account, the Administrator of the account can reset the password.
