---
title: "How are funds from successful transactions settled?"
sidebar_label: "How are funds from successful transactions settled?"
sidebar_position: 2
---

Upon fulfillment of the contractual settlement conditions, Oceanpayment will transfer the agreed settlement funds to the merchant's contracted bank account or the Opasst settlement platform.

If the contracted account is a bank account, you can check the corresponding transferred funds in that bank account.

If you have signed up for Opasst, you can log in to the corresponding system to withdraw funds or make payouts on behalf of the merchant.
