Effective on 31st March 2025, Visa will retire the Visa Dispute Monitoring Program (VDMP) and the Visa Fraud Monitoring Program (VFMP). And will enhance the Visa Acquirer Monitoring Program (VAMP) with effect from 1st April 2025.
